Creating a Puzzle

To create a puzzle, provide the dimensions of your puzzle and click on "Generate Board". Once you see the board, you can click or tap once on a tile to place an obstacle, and tap twice on a tile to place a player on the board. As you place the obstacles and the player, keep in mind that the player must be able to move over each grass tile on the board. When you're ready, click "Test Puzzle" to test your creation. Once you complete the level you've created, then it can be submitted for others to play!

Testing a Puzzle

When testing a puzzle, play through the level to ensure it's solvable. If the level is too difficult or impossible, you may have to return to the creation screen to make adjustments. Once the puzzle is complete and satisfactory, and you have successfully completed the level once, submit it for others to play!

Playing a Puzzle

To play a puzzle, swipe or use arrow keys to move the player in the desired direction. The player will continue to move in that direction until they hit an obstacle or the edge of the board. Mow over each grass tile on the board to complete the level!

How to Play Lawnscapers

The goal of Lawnscapers is to move over each of the grass tiles in as few moves as possible. In each level, you'll encounter different obstacles, such as walls and fences, that you'll need to navigate around.

Good luck and have fun!

Understanding the Leaderboards

The leaderboards display the top scores achieved by players on each level. You can access the leaderboards from the main menu by selecting either 'Official Levels' or 'Community Levels.' These sections showcase the best performances for each type of puzzle.

How to View Scores: Leaderboards are sorted by rank, showing the player's name and their achieved score. Scores are ordered from the lowest (best) to the highest. If you've played a level, look for your name to see how you rank among other players!

Submitting Your Score: Achieved a high score? It will automatically submit your high score to the leaderboards when you complete a level, so no worries about missing out on the glory!

Tips for High Scores: Plan your moves carefully and aim to complete levels using the fewest moves possible. Remember, lower scores indicate better performance!

Leaderboard Updates: Leaderboards are updated regularly to reflect new high scores. Keep playing and improving your skills to climb up the ranks!
