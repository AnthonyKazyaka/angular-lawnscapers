Creating a Puzzle

To create a puzzle, provide the dimensions of your puzzle and click on "Generate Board". Once you see the board, you can click or tap once on a tile to place an obstacle, and tap twice on a tile to place a player on the board. As you place the obstacles and the player, keep in mind that the player must be able to move over each grass tile on the board. When you're ready, click "Test Puzzle" to test your creation. Once you complete the level you've created, then it can be submitted for others to play!

Testing a Puzzle

When testing a puzzle, play through the level to ensure it's solvable. If the level is too difficult or impossible, you may have to return to the creation screen to make adjustments. Once the puzzle is complete and satisfactory, and you have successfully completed the level once, submit it for others to play!

Playing a Puzzle

To play a puzzle, swipe or use arrow keys to move the player in the desired direction. The player will continue to move in that direction until they hit an obstacle or the edge of the board. Mow over each grass tile on the board to complete the level!
